I am a postdoctoral researcher at Sapienza University of Rome. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Sapienza, where I was advised by Flavio Chierichetti and Alessandro Panconesi. My research focuses on online algorithms and theoretical foundations of machine learning and economics. Specifically, I have worked on stochastic online bipartite matching and learning algorithms for discrete choice models, including random utility models and multinomial-logit/Plackett-Luce models.

I am on the job market for postdoctoral positions starting in mid-to-late 2026.

I have served (or am serving) as PC member for KDD (2024), TheWebConf (2024, 2026), and AAAI (2026), and as reviewer for SODA (2025, 2026), ITCS (2025), ESA (2025), ICML (2026), KDD (2023), TheWebConf (2025), SDM (2024), and TIST.
